Food Blog

Simple layout for a food blog with cards for each recipe. There is a picture of the dish on the left and the list of ingredients to the right. New recipes can easily be added by creating new cards.

A navigation bar on top leads to an about, bio and contact page. On the bottom of the page is a button that leads back to the top.
