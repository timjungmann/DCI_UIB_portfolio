Spicy Braised Tofu

what you will need:

  • 1 14-oz. block firm or extra-firm tofu, drained
  • 3 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp. mirin (sweet Japanese rice wine)
  • 1 tbsp. toasted sesame oil
  • ¾ tbsp. gochugaru (coarse Korean red pepper powder)
  • 1 scallion
  • 2 tbsp. grapeseed or vegetable oil, divided, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 small garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp. finely chopped peeled ginger
  • kosher salt
  • toasted sesame seeds and cooked rice (for serving)

Chicken Phở

what you will need:

  • 2 medium white or yellow onions (roasted)
  • 20 g fresh ginger (roasted)
  • 2 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick (approximately 2 inches)
  • 2 star anise pods
  • 1 whole chicken (cut in half)
  • 16 c water
  • 2½ tbsp salt + 2 tbsp sugar
  • 16 oz (1 pack) dried phở noodles (small noodle thickness)
  • bean sprouts (preferably blanched)
  • thai basil
  • hoisin sauce

Kimchi Udon with Scallions

what you will need:

  • 3 tbsp. unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 cup finely chopped kimchi, plus ⅓ cup kimchi juice
  • 2 tbsp. gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste)
  • ½ cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 pound fresh or frozen udon noodles
  • 4 large egg yolks, room temperature
  • 3 scallions, white and pale-green parts only, thinly sliced on a diagonal
  • 1 tbsp. toasted sesame seeds
  • kosher salt
